About Us

We support all black-owned businesses! We are a group of sports executives, former Ivy League athletes, and marketing/event experts who are working together to instill change in our society. We aim to support and raise awareness for black-owned businesses in an effort to build a powerful infrastructure and expansive database for the black business community.

Our mission is to provide ongoing benefits and financial resources to the black owned business community. In these unprecedented times, we must come together as one unit to support our fellow brothers and sisters who are continuously working to find success within a partisan system. Through continued efforts, our goal is to compile a $1 million fund to be dispersed as grants to black owned businesses in need!
